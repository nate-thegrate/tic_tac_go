# Privacy Policy for tic-tac-go

**Last updated:** July 16, 2026

**Developer:** Nate Wilson

Tic-tac-go is a free offline board game. This policy explains how it handles information.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** This app does not collect personal information, require an account, or use advertising, analytics, or tracking.

## What stays on your device

Settings are saved locally so they persist between sessions. They are not uploaded. This may include:

- Visual mode and board size
- Player mode, AI difficulty, and ruleset
- Preferred player mark and tutorial completion

Clearing the app’s data or uninstalling it removes this information.

Gameplay (moves and board state) runs only on your device. Two-player mode is local — not online multiplayer.

## Third parties

App stores and code hosts (for example Google Play, the App Store, or GitHub) have their own privacy policies for downloads and related activity. We don’t control those.

## Children

We don’t collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If data practices change, we’ll update this policy and the date above.
